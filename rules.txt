Guess the WORDLE in 6 tries

After each guess, the colour of the tiles will 
change to show how close your guess
was to the word

-> First, go to options and select the 
     difficulty level of the word.
-> Next click on 'new game' and start
     guessing.
  -   The letters R and S are in the word
and in the correct spot
  -    The letters U and E are in the word
 but not in the right spot
  -    The letter L is not in the word
